Data is
everywhere.

Data is everywhere - woven through emotions, behavior, and every human interaction. That realization, born from my passion for photography and understanding people, led me to pursue a BSc in Data Science and AI at Christ University, Delhi NCR. I don't just study algorithms. I study people, societies, and the patterns that connect them; and I build things that reflect that curiosity.

Ranked #1 at C-DAC among 45+ trainees, built a Real Time face mask detection system using Transfer Learning, developed SmartSIP- an AI that suggests Top Up amount of for your SIP using LSTM forecasting and live news sentiment and I'm currently building a RAG-based knowledge system for Olympia Academia. Every project starts with a question, not a tool.

That same curiosity lives beyond the technical. I was selected to lead the Eulim Science Club at Christ University, where I led a team of 25+ students, organized national-level events for 1,500+ students, and drove 900K+ in digital engagement. I am also a Peer Trainer at Center for Health and Counselling Services; Christ University Delhi NCR; because understanding human behavior isn't just something I study, it's something I practice.

Right now, I'm a McKinsey Forward Program fellow and a Round 2 UN Millennium Fellowship applicant, where I'm building the Bihar Agri-Intelligence Hub; an interactive platform visualizing 58 years of Bihar's agricultural data. I believe everything is connected by data. If you're working at the intersection of AI, human behavior, and real-world impact-let's connect.
